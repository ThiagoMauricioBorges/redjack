The first player to reach 100 points or more in their total score wins the game.

How to Play:

  • The game is played in turns. On your turn, you roll the dice.
  • The number you roll is added to your current score.
  • After rolling, you have two options:
    • Roll again: If you choose to roll again, the new number will be added to your current score.
    • Keep your points: If you choose to stop, your current score is added to your total score, and your turn ends. The next player takes their turn.
  • Important Rule: If you roll a 1, you lose all the points in your current score for that turn, and your turn ends immediately. The next player takes their turn.

Winning the Game: The first player to reach 100 points or more in total score wins the game.

Good luck to everyone!
